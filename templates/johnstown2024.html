Johnstown 2024

Welcome to the inaugural event of 0K: The Finish Line Moves in Johnstown this 2024

A beacon of hope and solidarity in our shared journey towards suicide prevention, this banner year, we gather in hope, remembrance, and celebration for those affected by suicide.

Together, we can all be Life-Savers!

You are not alone!

Join us in creating a ripple of change that extends beyond this day—become a participant, form a team, or step forward as a sponsor. Together, we can build a community of support, understanding, and action.

We invite community members, local influencers, and potential sponsors to learn more about our cause and discover how you can contribute to this vital movement. Your involvement can make a world of difference, offering hope and healing to many. Be part of something truly impactful. Together, let’s celebrate life, encourage one another, and stand united to prevent suicide. Join us in our mission to save lives and spread a message of love and support throughout our community and beyond.

Trophy Announcement

The participant, team, and sponsor raising the most money will be awarded a trophy!

“Champions of Hope” Awards

Celebrate the spirit of giving with our “Champions of Hope” trophies, awarded to the participant, team, and sponsor who go above and beyond in their fundraising efforts. Your remarkable contributions light the way to a brighter future, and we want to honor your commitment and impact with a symbol of our gratitude and recognition.

“Heart of the Community” Trophy

This special accolade goes to the participant, team, and sponsor whose extraordinary fundraising achievements embody the heart and soul of our mission. Your dedication not only raises crucial funds but also amplifies our collective voice against suicide, making you true heroes in our community.

“Beacons of Light” Recognition

Shine bright as a beacon of light! The top fundraiser from each category—individual, team, and sponsor—will receive a prestigious “Beacons of Light” trophy, symbolizing your role in illuminating the path to hope and support for those in need. Let’s celebrate your inspiring efforts and success in bringing us closer to a world without suicide.

“Guardians of Tomorrow” Awards

Acknowledging the forward-looking impact of your fundraising, the “Guardians of Tomorrow” trophies will be presented to those who lead the way in contributions. Your visionary support as a participant, team, or sponsor safeguards our future, ensuring ongoing care and prevention efforts. Join us in honoring these guardians who pave the way for enduring change.

“Unity in Action” Trophies

In recognition of the collective effort and unity it takes to make a difference, the “Unity in Action” trophies will be awarded to the highest fundraisers. This honor celebrates not just the funds raised but the spirit of collaboration and solidarity you’ve shown. Together, you’re a formidable force for good, and we’re thrilled to commemorate your achievement.

Social Media Spotlight

Winners will not only receive a trophy but also be featured in a special social media campaign. This will include interviews, stories, and posts celebrating your journey, achievements, and the difference you’ve made. It’s an opportunity to shine in the spotlight and inspire others with your dedication and success.
