Welcome to the inaugural event

0K: The Finish Line Moves in Johnstown this 2024

Join us in the fight against suicide! Together, we're beacons of hope, shining brightly in our shared journey towards prevention. You're not alone in this struggle; together, we can be Life-Savers, extending our hands to those in need and offering support in times of darkness.

Let's create ripples of change together, starting with this moment. Your participation, whether as a team member, sponsor, or advocate, is vital in building a community of understanding and action. Together, we can ignite a movement that reaches far beyond this day, spreading love and healing to countless lives.
