Johnstown 2024

Welcome to the inaugural event of 0K: The Finish Line Moves in Johnstown this 2024

A beacon of hope and solidarity in our shared journey towards suicide prevention, this banner year, we gather in hope, remembrance, and celebration for those affected by suicide.

Together, we can all be Life-Savers!

You are not alone!

Join us in creating a ripple of change that extends beyond this day—become a participant, form a team, or step forward as a sponsor. Together, we can build a community of support, understanding, and action.

We invite community members, local influencers, and potential sponsors to learn more about our cause and discover how you can contribute to this vital movement. Your involvement can make a world of difference, offering hope and healing to many. Be part of something truly impactful. Together, let’s celebrate life, encourage one another, and stand united to prevent suicide. Join us in our mission to save lives and spread a message of love and support throughout our community and beyond.
