About us

The 0K Foundation, established in 2023 by Patch Wetzel, is dedicated to supporting individuals grappling with mental illness by raising crucial funds for existing organizations dedicated to mental health advocacy and support.

At 0K Foundation, we recognize the immense challenges faced by those navigating the complexities of mental health disorders. Our mission is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves access to comprehensive mental health care and support services.

Driven by a commitment to fostering tangible change, we channel our efforts into fundraising initiatives aimed at bolstering the resources and capabilities of established organizations already making significant strides in mental health advocacy and support. Through strategic partnerships and community engagement, we strive to amplify the impact of these organizations, ensuring that they have the necessary resources to continue their vital work.

At the core of our ethos lies a deep-seated belief in the power of collective action. By mobilizing individuals, businesses, and communities, we endeavor to spark conversations, raise awareness, and break down barriers surrounding mental health. Together, we aim to cultivate a more compassionate and supportive society where individuals facing mental health challenges are met with understanding, empathy, and access to the resources they need to thrive.

Join us in our mission to champion mental health awareness, support, and advocacy. Together, we can make a difference—one step, one contribution, one individual at a time.
