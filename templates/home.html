About Us

Our Mission

The 0K Foundation fuels mental health support by fundraising for existing advocacy organizations. We believe in universal access to mental health care and champion tangible change through partnerships and community engagement.

Our Vision

We envision a society where mental health is prioritized and supported for all. By amplifying existing organizations and sparking conversations, we aim to create a compassionate community where everyone has access to the resources they need to thrive.
