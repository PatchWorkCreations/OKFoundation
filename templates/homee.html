Our Mission

The 0K Foundation fuels mental health support by fundraising for existing advocacy organizations. We believe in universal access to mental health care and champion tangible change through partnerships and community engagement.

Our Vision

The 0K Foundation is dedicated to raising funds to support existing organizations in their mission to eliminate suicide as an option for everyone. By providing resources to these communities, we aim to strengthen their efforts in suicide prevention and mental health support. Together, we're working towards a future where every person feels valued, supported, and knows that suicide is never the answer. Join us in making this vision a reality by contributing to our cause today.
